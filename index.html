About & methodology Research guides

Accuracy: Currentry's viewpoint labels (left / center / right) are editorial approximations informed by independent media-bias research — aids for balance, not definitive judgments. The live news feed is gathered automatically from public sources, so headlines and groupings can occasionally be off. Read across viewpoints and give anything important a human check (how the labels work).

Your privacy — nothing is stored on a server: Currentry runs entirely in your browser. There are no accounts, and what you do here is never tracked, collected, or stored — on a server or on your device. The News page only loads a small data file from this site itself and makes no third-party requests. Links you follow lead to outside publishers and government sites, which keep their own privacy practices.

Built with AI: This tool was created with the assistance of AI (Anthropic's Claude). It uses no AI engine to run — everything you see is plain, static code and data.

Currentry — a self-contained, viewpoint-balanced guide for public policy professionals, students, and the curious.
